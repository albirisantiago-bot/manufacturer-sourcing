## ğŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### Baltic States:
- **Best for:** Tech/digital companies, Nordic market, small-medium orders
- **Language:** English widely spoken (70-95%)
- **Currency:** Euro (all three)
- **Customs:** None (EU + Schengen)
- **Timing:** Plan around Baltic summer (June-August) - capacity tight

### Balkan States:
- **Best for:** Large volumes, cost-sensitive projects, tourism packaging
- **Language:** English varies (30-70%), consider translator for non-EU
- **Currency:** Mixed (Euro in Slovenia/Greece, local in others)
- **Customs:** EU members = no customs; Non-EU = customs clearance needed
- **Timing:** Avoid Orthodox holidays (different calendar)

### EU vs Non-EU:
- **EU members:** No customs, VAT reverse charge, free movement
- **Non-EU candidates:** Customs needed, pre-accession benefits, lower costs
